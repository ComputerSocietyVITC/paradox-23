Paradox

About

  • PARADOX is a two day online cryptic hunt organised by IEEE Computer Society of VIT Chennai.
  • Attended by over 500 people in 2022, PARADOX is FREE and open for anyone, in any college.
  • The hunt consists of several questions in the form of seemingly random pictures and text from which players will have to deduce the answer by scouring the internet as well as their brains.
  • The goal of the players is to submit the answer as fast as possible to stay on top of the leaderboard.
  • Check out our instagram for solutions to previous editions of PARADOX and other details.

Rules

  • At each level, the participants will encounter a number of clues all of which together point to one final answer. You may use any internet resource to find the answer.
  • Each level has only one correct answer.
  • It is compulsory for all participants to join our discord server. All official hints will be released on the discord server.
  • Answers will always be in lower-case, alphanumeric and will contain no spaces. Special characters are not allowed.
  • Beware of the spelling you enter. For example, if the answer is 221-B Baker Street, you would type it in as "221bbakerstreet" (without the quotes).
  • Collaborating with other competing participants is not allowed and any such activity can lead to disqualification of the people involved.
